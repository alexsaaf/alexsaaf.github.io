Super Platformer on Google Play Store

I have just released my first game ever on the Google Play Store! It is a small mobile platforming game where the player must make their way through rooms filled with dangers. The rooms are randomly drawn from a predefined set of rooms, and upon death the player needs to start over from the first room. The game might not be very large, but I am fairly happy with how it turned out!
